FLORIST

Florists work at flower shops to prepare and sell bouquets, flowering plants, corsages and freshly cut flower stems.
They manage customer service by answering phones, greeting customers, taking orders and coordinating deliveries.
Florists arrange displays in the store and organize flowers by color, type or occasion.
They gather flowers into appealing combinations for gifts and decor, carefully packaging bouquets to preserve freshness and extend the shelf life of the flowers.
